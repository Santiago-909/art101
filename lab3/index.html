Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem Solving

Challenge

The challenge of the lab was to use Pseudocoding to prepare a Rock, Paper, Scissors game in JS. We had to use critial thinking and our problem solving skills.

Problems

The problems that arose were that we had to really think granullarly about which tasks required subtasks and such.

Reflection

This was a nice assigment because it helped us think more critically of every day tasks. I feel that this type of coding is helpful in real life when you have a lot of tasks that you can break down into little ones.
